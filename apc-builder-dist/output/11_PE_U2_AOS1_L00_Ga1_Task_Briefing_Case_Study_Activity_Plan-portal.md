# Portal Continuum Tasks — GA1 Task Briefing — Case Study Activity Plan

*Physical Education · Unit 2 AOS 1 — Outcome 1 (Case Study Edition)*

## Case study: judge & evaluate a plan
**Time:** 20 minutes

### Acquiring
Using the netball practice log, identify two days where the moderate-to-vigorous guideline was met and two where it wasn't.

### Consolidating
Describe how the netball player's screen time compares to the recreational guideline across the week, using her calculated totals.

### Refining
Explain the contradiction between what the netball player believes about her activity levels and what her calculated data shows, using specific evidence.

### Extending
Evaluate whether a plan built from this data would need to focus more on increasing MVPA or reducing screen time, justifying your priority with the SEM.

### Mastering
Design and justify a two-week SMARTER goal sequence for the netball player, integrating guideline analysis, enabler/barrier categorisation by SEM level, and predicted adherence challenges.
