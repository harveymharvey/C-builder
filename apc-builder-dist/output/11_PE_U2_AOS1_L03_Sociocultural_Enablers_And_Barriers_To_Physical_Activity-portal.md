# Portal Continuum Tasks — Sociocultural Enablers and Barriers to Physical Activity

*Physical Education · Unit 2 AOS 1 — Sociocultural Influences*

## Enablers & Barriers — Your Sport
**Time:** 20 minutes

### Acquiring
Choose ONE sociocultural factor (family, peers, gender, SES, community or cultural norms). Identify ONE enabler and ONE barrier it could create for someone starting AFL (or your own sport).

### Consolidating
Choose ONE sociocultural factor. Describe how it could act as BOTH an enabler and a barrier for a Year 9 student starting AFL (or your own sport), using full sentences.

### Refining
Choose TWO sociocultural factors. Explain how each could enable AND limit participation in AFL for a teenager from a low-income family, linking your answer to specific examples.

### Extending
Discuss how THREE sociocultural factors interact to shape whether a teenage girl from a non-English-speaking family takes up AFLW. Consider both enablers and barriers together.

### Mastering
A local council wants to increase AFL participation among a specific under-represented group (e.g. girls, low-SES families, First Nations students). Design and justify TWO strategies, explaining which sociocultural barriers each strategy removes.
