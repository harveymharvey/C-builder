# Portal Continuum Tasks — Types of Physical Activity

*Physical Education · Unit 2 AOS 1 — Physical Activity*

## Classify Your Own Week of Activity
**Time:** 15 minutes

### Acquiring
Identify TWO physical activities from your own week and name which type each belongs to (play, games, formal or informal sport, transport, exercise, chores, or recreational).

### Consolidating
Describe THREE physical activities from your week, naming the type of each and outlining ONE characteristic that makes it that type.

### Refining
Choose a soccer session from your week (training, match or backyard kick-to-kick) and explain why it is formal or informal sport, using the characteristics from Table 7.1.

### Extending
Compare a formal soccer match and an informal backyard kick-to-kick game, discussing similarities and differences across at least THREE characteristics (organisation, rules, venue).

### Mastering
A local council wants to increase soccer participation among people who can't afford formal club fees. Recommend ONE informal soccer activity they could set up, and justify why it removes the socioeconomic barrier while still counting as meaningful physical activity.
