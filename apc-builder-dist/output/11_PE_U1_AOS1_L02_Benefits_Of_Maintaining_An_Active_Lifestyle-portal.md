# Portal Continuum Tasks — Benefits of Maintaining an Active Lifestyle

*VCE Physical Education · Unit 1 AOS 1*

## Netball Benefits Continuum
**Time:** 20 minutes

### Acquiring
Identify ONE benefit of regular netball participation from each of the five dimensions (physical, mental, emotional, social, spiritual). Use the textbook headings to help.

### Consolidating
Describe ONE benefit from each of the five dimensions, stating what happens to the netball player's body or mind for each.

### Refining
Explain how netball participation produces a benefit in THREE of the five dimensions, describing the mechanism (e.g. hormone, relationship, physical adaptation) for each.

### Extending
Discuss how a netball player's benefits might differ across the physical, mental, emotional, social and spiritual dimensions if she plays for a school team versus a community club.

### Mastering
A netball player is considering quitting the sport due to a minor injury. Predict which dimensions of benefit she would lose access to, and justify which loss would most affect her overall wellbeing.
