# Portal Continuum Tasks — Physical Activity & Sedentary Behaviour Guidelines

*Physical Education*

## Lifespan Guidelines Continuum — Swimming
**Time:** 20 minutes

### Acquiring
Identify the daily physical activity guideline for a 5-year-old learning to swim and for a 70-year-old hydrotherapy client.

### Consolidating
Describe the physical activity and sedentary behaviour guidelines for a 15-year-old competitive swimmer, including the strength/bone and screen-time components.

### Refining
Explain why a 35-year-old masters swimmer's weekly activity minutes differ from a 70-year-old's, using the guidelines' age bands as evidence.

### Extending
Evaluate whether a swim club's typical junior program (three 60-minute squad sessions per week) meets the guideline for a 10-year-old, addressing both the activity and screen-time components.

### Mastering
Design and justify a week-long activity plan for a family with an infant, a teenager and a grandparent that meets each member's guideline using swimming and related aquatic activity, and predict which family member is most at risk of falling short and why.
